# PageBrief — Product Spec（P1）

## Positioning

Upload a PDF → get a one-page structured brief with section map + cited bullets.  
SEO-led growth（less viral than image tools）.

## Why it lives

- Search intent: “summarize PDF”, “PDF briefing”
- Sticky: history + private docs → subscription
- Different growth channel vs SnapShelf/FacePass（矩阵对冲）

## Scope

1. Upload PDF ≤ 20 pages（free tier）
2. Output: outline + key bullets with page citations
3. Free: 1 doc/day, watermark footer on export
4. Pro: more pages, history, no watermark

## Out of scope

- Full RAG chat over enterprise corpora
- Multi-user team workspace（v2）

## Landing copy（EN）

- H1: Turn any PDF into a cited one-page brief
- Sub: Free daily trial. Subscribe for longer docs and history.
- CTA: Upload PDF

## Channels

- SEO landing pages
- Product Hunt
- Reddit: r/GradSchool, r/productivity, r/datascience

## Success metric（14 days）

- Organic search visits + ≥ 1 Pro checkout OR email capture ≥ 50
