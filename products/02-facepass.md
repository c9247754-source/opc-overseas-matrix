# FacePass — Product Spec（P0）

## Positioning

Upload selfies → get professional LinkedIn / ID-style headshots.  
Free previews with watermark.

## Why it lives

- Photo AI / similar tools proved willingness to pay
- Output is used as avatar → natural distribution
- Same Freemium skeleton as SnapShelf（复用代码）

## Scope

1. Upload 1–4 selfies
2. Generate 4 style variants（professional / casual / studio / friendly）
3. Free: watermarked grid download
4. Paid: clean pack download

## Out of scope

- Video, full body fashion, dating-app spam features
- Fine-tune private LoRA（成本高，v2 再说）

## Landing copy（EN）

- H1: Professional headshots from your selfies
- Sub: Try free. Upgrade when you need clean, watermark-free photos.
- CTA: Upload selfies

## Channels

- Product Hunt
- Reddit: r/jobs, r/LinkedInLain, r/CareerGuidance
- Watermark on profile pics（弱但有）

## Success metric（14 days）

- ≥ 1 paid pack OR strong repeat visits same fingerprint
